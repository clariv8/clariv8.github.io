Frequently Asked Questions

How much time and effort will this require from me?

You get to decide.

It is entirely up to you and your availability. When we contact you for feedback, you get to decide:

  • How much time you can spare.
  • When it is best/suitable for you to engage with us.
  • What knowledge areas, features, ideas or type of research you want to influence.
  • You do not have to respond if you are not interested.

What type of research will I be involved in?

Depending on what we are working on, research activities may include:

  • Usability Testing
  • Interviews
  • Surveys
  • Workshops
  • Show & Tell
Most research activities take place remotely from the comfort of your own computer

What will you do with my feedback  and information?

With your prior permission, we record all research sessions.
However, all findings are for internal use only. We will not disclose any personally or business information. Furthermore, you have the right to ask us to remove all information related to you.

What if I no longer want to be  part of the panel?

You can always ask us to remove you from the User Research Panel. All communications will also offer you an option to opt out.

I have more questions or comments, how can I contact you?

Do feel free to email the user experience team, customer care or support.
